Wii U Gamepad Redesign


Introduction: The Gamepad is the primary method of input and interface on Kyoto-based Nintendo Co. Ltd.'s Wii U console. A render of the Gamepad can be found on their website. The goal of this project for my Human Factors in Design course was to identify shortcomings in an existing consumer technology, and to design a solution that addresses these pitfalls.
